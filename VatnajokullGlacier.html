Vatnajokull Glacier Cave,Iceland

Located within Iceland,s glaciers, the ice caves are totally magical formations that burst with vibrant hues and patterns of blue,white,gray & black.Filled with world of different textures, light passes through the walls to create fascinating compositions.These impressive passages of raw & untouched ice contain a spellbinding beauty like no other.

Walking through one is breathtaking adventure that will have you in awe from start to finish, and is an experience that you will definetely remember for a very long time.You will be literally in the heart of a glacier that has formed thousand years ago.

Although Summer in Iceland promises endless days & warmer weather, the winters are just as,if not more exciting.From around November to March each year, Ice Caves season is in full swing & exploration of these glorious artworks of nature can be experienced & photographed safely.



What makes the ice caves even more precious, is that they are ever-changing-they shift in shape daily. Here

one week, they could be gone the next.Each season reveals several never before seen passages with new

caves being discovered frequently.So what you experience will be completely unique, and entirely upto

Mother Nature.

The glacier advances in the winter & melts in the summer.The melt-water from the warmer months & sunlight

cave channels into the glacier ice, forming rivers that eventually create tunnels through the bottom of the glacier.




Glaciers are beautiful & attract many tourists each year,however, its beauty can only be

matched by its danger.We highly advise that if planning to visit an ice cave in Iceland, you

do it with right equipment & with experienced guides or book a tour with a reputed

glacier tour company.Itis true that in Iceland there is now law to forbid you from

exploring the glacier on your own, however, this is where common sense should kick in.
