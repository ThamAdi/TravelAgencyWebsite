Glowworm Caves, New Zeland

The Waitomo Glowworm Caves attraction is a cave at Waitomo on the North Island of New Zeland.The Waitomo Glowworm Caves are located in the southern Waikato region of

the North Island of New Zeland, 12km Northwest of Te Kuiti.This cave is about 2 hours west of Rotorua by car.The directions to caves are - to exit State highway3 onto Waitomo

Caves Road & to continue on the road for about 8km.It is known for its population of ARACHNOCAMPA LUMINOSA, a glowworm species found exclusively in New Zeland.


The attraction has a modern visitor centre at the entrance,largely designed in wood.There are organized tours that include a BOAT RIDE under the glowworms. This cave is a

part of the Waitomo Caves system that includes the Ruakuri Cave & the Aranui Cave.




World-renowned and a magnet for both local & overseas visitors,the Waitomo Glowworm Caves occupy a

high place in the New Zeland vacation wish-list. This Glowworm is unique to New Zeland & thousands of

these tiny creatures radiate their unmistakable luminescent light as the expert guides provide informative

commentary on the Caves' historical & geological significance.








Waitomo Glowworm Caves are a must-see for any traveller.Enjoy the most famous

boatride under thousands of MAGICAL GLOWWORMS & become a part of over 120

years of cultural & natural history.

Marvel at Mother Nature's light display as you glide silently through the starry

wonderland of the Glowworm Grotto.Meander underground along the Waitomo River &

gaze in silence at the myriad of glowworm lights that make up the Glowworm Grotto.As

you enter this galaxy of tiny living lights, you will immediately experience a serene

ambience & be fascinated & intrigued by tiny glowworms that light your way.
