Niagara Falls,USA

Located on the Niagara River, which drains Lake Erie into Lake Ontario, the combined falls form the highest fiow rate of any waterfall in North America.During peak day time

tourist hours, more than 6 million cubic feet of water goes over the crest of the falls every minute.


Niagara Falls are 3 massive waterfalls that firm the border between Canada & United States.The Falls were formed at the END OF THE LAST ICE AGE when the water from

Great Lakes broke through the Niagara Escarpment on the way to Atlantic Ocean.

Niagara Falls is famed both for its beauty & as a valuable source of hydroelectric power.





The Falls are a hugely popular tourist destination & have attracted families, & daredevils of all kinds,from

those who went down in a barrel to those stretched a wire over the falls & walked across it.The Falls have

inspired hundreds of artists to create wonderful art & are still inspiring the millions of people who come to

admire the majesty of nature as well as its power & its magnificent beauty.






Niagara Falls State Park is opened 365 days a year, Niagara Falls State Park has awe-

inspiring scenery!!!

It also features thrilling attractions, interactive exhibits, miles of hiking trails, &

delectable dining options in family-friendly environment.

Also ther are guided hikes and walks to talks about rich history of Niagara Falls,there are

events for the entire family to enjoy!!!
